Keisuke

Enterprise Services Consultant

About Keisuke

With over 10 years of living and working in Dubai, Keisuke has built deep expertise in the UAE business landscape. In 2021, he established his own trading company in Dubai, and has since become a trusted advisor for Japanese businesses looking to enter the UAE market.

Keisuke has successfully supported approximately 10 Japanese companies through the corporate establishment process in the UAE, navigating free zone regulations, licensing, and local market entry strategies.

In addition to his consultancy work, he runs a specialised wholesale business dealing in knives, tableware, and cooking utensils — and provides comprehensive business support and translation services between Japan and the UAE.

Career Profile

  • 10+ years living and working in Dubai, UAE
  • Established a trading company in Dubai in 2021
  • Supported ~10 Japanese companies in corporate establishment in UAE
  • Running a wholesale business for knives, tableware & cooking utensils
  • Business support & Japanese-Arabic/English translation services

ドバイ在住10年以上のキャリアを持ち、UAE市場に精通。2021年にドバイで自ら貿易会社を設立し、日本企業のUAE進出を支援するアドバイザーとして活動中。

これまでに日本企業約10社の法人設立を支援し、フリーゾーンの規制・ライセンス取得・現地市場参入戦略のナビゲーションを担っている。
