About Eriko

Eriko brings a decade of hands-on systems engineering experience from one of the world's leading foreign IT corporations, combined with entrepreneurial drive as the co-founder and operator of two companies.

As CTO and Head of Operations at We Raise Investment, Eriko is responsible for the firm's technology infrastructure and day-to-day operational management, ensuring the highest standards of efficiency and security.

Since 2022, Eriko has been actively expanding her expertise into crypto asset investment, applying her analytical systems-thinking approach to the digital asset space and investing her personal assets accordingly.
