About Ayaka

After transitioning from homemaker to entrepreneur, Ayaka has built an impressive career spanning beauty product development, manufacturing, sales, and international export — with businesses established across Japan, France, and Poland.

Drawing on her passion for self-immunity and regenerative science, she independently developed proprietary beauty technology and founded a medical aesthetic salon offering personalised care. Her business has since expanded from a beauty salon into a full medical clinic.

In June 2025, Ayaka established a fund company in Dubai. Through diversified asset management across crypto assets, real estate, and equities, she provides investors with tailored solutions that balance diversification and risk management.

Key Highlights

  • Entrepreneur with international business presence in Japan, France & Poland
  • Regenerative Medicine Clinic Advisor
  • Beauty product development, manufacturing, sales & export
  • Founder of a medical aesthetic salon
  • Mother of 3 — preparing to establish an NPO & public interest foundation for next-generation education
  • Co-founder, We Raise Investment LLC — Dubai, 2025
